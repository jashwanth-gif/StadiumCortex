# StadiumCortex Volunteer SOPs & Incident Protocols

## SOP-01: Lost Child Protocol (Code Pink)
1. **Immediate Comfort:** Keep the child calm and safe at your current location. Do not move the child immediately.
2. **Local Broadcast:** Use your radio to announce "Code Pink" to the Sector Supervisor, giving the child's description (clothing, age, gender, name if known).
3. **Wait 5 Minutes:** Stand by for 5 minutes to see if parents return to the immediate vicinity.
4. **Reunification Escort:** If parents do not return, escort the child to the **Family & Lost Desk** located in **Zone B, Main Lobby near Section 122**. 
5. **Form Filing:** Ensure the Family Desk Coordinator logs the child's details and initiates a stadium-wide staff announcement.

## SOP-02: Medical Incident Protocol (Code Blue)
1. **Assess the Situation:** Determine if the person is conscious and breathing. Do not attempt to move the casualty unless they are in immediate danger.
2. **Escalation Levels:**
   - **Level 1 (Minor Injury/Disorientation):** Escort or guide the patient to the **Medical Center A** (Zone C, Lower Ground level under Section 115) or dispatch a mobile first-aid team.
   - **Level 2 (Severe/Urgent Medical Emergency):** Call Emergency Services via radio or dial the control room dispatcher immediately. Give precise Sector and Section coordinates (e.g., "Zone B, Section 124, Row K"). Keep the patient warm and stable.
3. **Crowd Management:** Clear a 3-meter radius around the patient to allow medical staff to work and maintain patient privacy.

## SOP-03: Crowd Congestion & Gate Overflows
1. **Monitor Wait Times:** If Gate wait times exceed 10 minutes (such as current wait at **Gate B (North Gate)**), notify your Sector Supervisor.
2. **Directional Guiding:** Actively guide fans arriving near congested areas toward **Gate C (West Gate)** or **Gate D (South Gate)**, which have minimal queues.
3. **Signage Deployment:** Deploy portable "Alternative Entrance" sandwich-board signs to redirect incoming fan streams.

## SOP-04: Wheelchair & Elderly Support
1. **Step-Free Transit:** When guiding visitors with mobility devices, strollers, or physical limitations, direct them strictly through **Gate C (West)** or **Gate D (South)**.
2. **Elevator Priority:** Ensure that elevators located near Section 121 are reserved exclusively for guests with disabilities, seniors, and families with infant strollers.
3. **Escort Service:** If requested, radio the **Accessibility Help Point** in Zone A to dispatch a sighted guide or wheelchair assistant.

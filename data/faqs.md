# StadiumCortex Fan FAQs & Grounding Guide

## Navigation & Reaching Your Seat
- **Q: How do I reach my gate?**
  - **A:** If your ticket is for Sections 101-110, use **Gate A (East Gate)**. If your ticket is for Sections 121-130, use **Gate C (West Gate)**. For Sections 131-140, use **Gate D (South Gate)**. Gate B (North Gate) serves Sections 111-120 but currently has **High Congestion (12 min wait)**. We suggest entering through Gate C (West Gate) or Gate D (South Gate) and walking around the concourse if possible.
- **Q: How do I get back to my seat?**
  - **A:** Concourse signs are color-coded: **Green** for Lower Tier (Sections 101-140), **Blue** for Upper Tier (Sections 201-240). Check the section number on your ticket, follow the colored wall markers, or ask a Volunteer at any help point.

## Restrooms & Facilities
- **Q: Where is the nearest restroom?**
  - **A:** 
    - In **Concourse Level 1, near Section 104**, we have an All-Gender & Family Restroom with infant changing tables (Accessible, Low Queue: 1 min wait).
    - In **Concourse Level 2, near Section 118**, there is a Standard Restroom (Not accessible, High Queue: 10 min wait).
    - In **Concourse Level 2, near Section 128**, there is an Accessible Coed Restroom with motion-activated doors (Accessible, Moderate Queue: 4 min wait).
  
## Food & Dining
- **Q: Which food stall is less crowded?**
  - **A:** **Burger Pitch** (Concourse Level 2, near Section 112) is currently the least crowded with only a **3-minute wait**. **Stadia Brews** (Concourse Level 3, near Section 205) also has a low **2-minute wait**. Avoid **Taco Goal** (Concourse Level 1, Section 125) as it currently has a high **15-minute wait**.

## Accessibility Support
- **Q: I am a wheelchair user. What are the best routes?**
  - **A:** Avoid **Gate B (North Gate)** as it has steep escalators and no direct elevator access. Enter via **Gate C (West Gate)** or **Gate D (South Gate)**, which have level ramp access. Elevators are located next to Gate C to easily transition to Concourse Levels 2 and 3. Accessible restrooms are located at Section 104 and Section 128.
- **Q: Is there stroller parking or assistance?**
  - **A:** Yes! Families can park strollers and get identification wristbands at the **Family & Lost Desk** located in Zone B, Main Lobby near Section 122.

## Emergencies & Evacuation
- **Q: What should I do in an emergency?**
  - **A:** Remain calm. Follow the illuminated green exit signs and instructions from on-site security and public announcements. Proceed to your nearest exit gate: **Gate C (West)** and **Gate D (South)** have wide, step-free evacuation pathways. Do not use elevators or escalators in the event of an evacuation.
